Your Community Garden

Purpose

Green front community garden is a place where community can flourish and produce so many great things. Our focus involves providing a space to embrace gardening skills while also promoting healthy eating. We encourage everyone to come out and spend as little as ten minutes of your day to help with a little planting. Whether you are taking your daily walk or just having a family day outing, stop by to see your community garden's progress. Everyone is welcome to the yield of your Garden. As with most things in life just keep others in mind. No food goes to waste in our Garden, so donating fresh vegetables is our way of helping others in need.

We look forward to planting a variety vegetables and herbs each year that can be shared within our community. Green Front Community Garden prides itself on having sponsor's that care about community. In addition, to have sponsor's we like to invest in our Seed Saving Project, which allows us to "up-cycle" seeds for next year planting. Starting the process of maintaining our garden will evolve as the need for more resources grow, so we would greatly appreciate any ideas.
