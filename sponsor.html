Green Front - Neighborhood Garden

Community gardens can be classified based on their focus and purpose. Green Front Community Garden is condsidered a neighborhood garden. Neighbors work together to keep garden up and running and share produce. In addition to serving the local community we donate fruits and vegetables to our local shelters and food pantries. Some other type of community gardens are:

  • Donation Gardens
  • School Gardents
  • Therapy Gardents
  • Market Gardents
  • Allotment Gardens

Source:http://urbanharvest.org/typesofgardens
