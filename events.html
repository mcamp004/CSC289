Each month we will celebrate the growth in the Garden by putting on several events. These special events will not only highlight the progress of our garden but also add a social element. Growing vegetables to place healthy options at the dinner table, is now a convenient option. We encourage parents to bring their kids, to get them familiar with many varieties of vegetables. The community garden will also have varieties of herbs and flowers to bring our garden full circle. The elements created throughout the garden inspires our special events. Our canvas (garden) has exponential potential of lush foliage and veggies.

Event Date Description
Paint Pots 02-June Bring out the paint brushes and lets's paint pots
Start Seedlings 11-June Let's start together and plant seeds
Survey Vegetables 13-June Prune and count vegetables
Plant A Flower! 16-June Let's Create Color in our Garden with flowers
Compost Event 21-June Have organic material;bring it!
What Bug? 26-June Drawing event for kids to draw their favorite bug

Green Front Community Garden provides a way for people to serve the surrounding communities and the environment. Carving out spaces to grow garden enhances the soil and creates great ecosystems for pollination. Not to mention, gardening is considered to be therapeutic. Each month we will update the event postings. We also send out pretty digital flyers, straight to your inbox. If you know a company or run one, and you like to sponsor an event contact us!
