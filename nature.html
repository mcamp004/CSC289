Tomatillo Plant

Photo: Bumblebee enjoying some nectar in Green Front Community Garden.-2017

Info: Native to Mexico, the Tomatillo is also called Mexican ground cherry or Mexican husk tomato. The fruit has a tart-fruity taste and is used mostly in salsas(salsa verde) and sauces.

Planting: Tomatillos are great for summer gardens
