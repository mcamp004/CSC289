New Fruit/Vegetable Variety

Plan for Spring/Summer Garden

Every year we like to add new varieties fruits and vegetables to give use idea about what produce is ideal for our particular garden. We have decides on four new varieties for our summer garden which include: Brandywine Pink Tomato, Black Beauty Zucchini, Purple Podded Pole Bean, and Mary Washington Asparagus. We look forward to charting these particular varieties growth over the next few weeks.

Brandywine Pink Tomato

Description: Heirloom standard dating back to 1885(beefsteak-fruit)

  • Requires Full Sun
  • Seedlings emerge in 7-14 days
  • Days to Maturity: 85 days

Black Beauty Zucchini

Description: A summer squash with white flesh and dark green skin.

  • Requires Full Sun
  • Fruit Size: 6-8 in.
  • Seedlings emerge in 10-14 days
  • Days To Maturity: 50 days
